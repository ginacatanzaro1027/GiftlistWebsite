Places to Visit

  • New York City: Visit landmarks like Times Square and Central Park, enjoy Broadway shows, and explore diverse food options.
  • Paris: Famous for the Eiffel Tower, beautiful architecture, art museums, and amazing pastries.
  • Tokyo: Unique culture, delicious sushi, cutting-edge technology, and beautiful cherry blossoms in spring.
  • Grand Canyon: Stunning natural views, hiking trails, and breathtaking scenery that’s perfect for photos.
  • Rome: Rich in history, the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and delicious Italian cuisine await travelers.
