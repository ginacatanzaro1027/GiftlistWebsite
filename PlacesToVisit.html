Places to Visit

  • New York City: Amazing landmarks like Times Square and Central Park.
  • Paris: Beautiful architecture, the Eiffel Tower, and delicious pastries.
  • Tokyo: Unique culture, technology, and amazing food.
  • Grand Canyon: Stunning natural views and great hiking opportunities.
  • Rome: Rich history, Colosseum, and Italian cuisine.
